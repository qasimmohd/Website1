About Us

Abu Dhabi's first dedicated board games cafe concept, Eat Play DNA first opened in the Al Khalidiya neighbourhood in December 2021. Offering over 200 fun board games for all ages (with new additions constantly being added), this Emirati and expat-owned cafe is the brainchild of two entrepreneurs who love to play board games.

There's a buzzy atmosphere, with luxury retro vibes and furnishings. A private games room for up to 14 people can be hired (for a minimum spend), and there are various seating arrangements for all group sizes. Drinks-wise, there are signature mocktails and specialty coffee powered by Taff and Archers, alongside milkshakes and soft drinks.

Food-wise, the menu is divided into breakfast, starters, smoothie bowls, mains, sourdough toasts, side dishes, salads and desserts.

Each table is assigned a games master, who will introduce your selected game, explaining the rules and process. There is a small charge for the games (19 AED for 1 hour, 29 AED for 2 hours, and an all-day rate at 39 AED).

Our mission is to create a space where people can disconnect from digital distractions and connect with each other through the joy of board games. Whether you're a seasoned player or new to the world of board games, our friendly staff is here to help you find the perfect game and make your experience memorable.
